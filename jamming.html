Chicago Scottish Jam

In collaboration with the Illinois St. Andrew Society, Tim is leading a Scottish jam session on certain Tuesdays.

All sessions will take place at the Duke of Perth in Lincoln Park (2913 N Clark St), begin at 7PM, and run until 9 or so. Everyone is encouraged to play their favorite Scottish tunes, but newcomers to the Scottish style might find it useful to look over the collection of tunes we’ll probably play.
